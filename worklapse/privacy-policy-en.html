Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 13, 2025

Worklapse ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled within the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information.

All timelapse videos recorded with the App are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

2. Camera Usage

The App uses your device's camera to record timelapse videos during work sessions. The camera footage is used solely for creating timelapse videos and is utilized for the following purposes:

  • Recording timelapse videos synchronized with the Pomodoro timer
  • Saving recorded videos to your device

3. Photo Library Access

The App requests write access to your Photo Library to save recorded timelapse videos. This access is used only for saving videos and does not read your existing photos or videos.

4. Data Storage

All data created by the App (videos, settings, etc.) is stored only on your device.

  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No synchronization with cloud services
  • No data sharing with third parties

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use analytics tools, advertising networks, or any other third-party services.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us: yorihide.saito@gmail.com
